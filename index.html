Privacy Policy for Chate VPN

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

Chate VPN (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use Chate VPN.

1. Information We Collect

Chate VPN may collect the following information:

  • Account information: username, login token, subscription status, and account validity.
  • Server usage information: selected VPN server, connection status, and remaining data or plan limits.
  • Payment or top-up information: payment request details submitted inside the app, such as amount, plan, and status.
  • Device and app information: app version, device type, and Firebase Cloud Messaging token for notifications.

2. VPN Traffic

Chate VPN does not inspect, read, or store the content of your VPN traffic.

We do not log:

  • Websites you visit
  • DNS queries
  • Content of your internet traffic
  • Messages, browsing content, or downloaded files

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide VPN access
  • Manage user accounts and subscriptions
  • Show available servers
  • Process top-up or payment requests
  • Send service notifications
  • Improve app reliability and security

4. Firebase and Notifications

Chate VPN uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to send app notifications. Firebase may collect device identifiers or notification tokens required to deliver notifications.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited information only when required to:

  • Provide the VPN service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our users, systems, and service security

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We keep account and service-related data only as long as necessary to provide the service, manage subscriptions, resolve disputes, or comply with legal requirements.

8. Children’s Privacy

Chate VPN is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. User Rights

Users may contact us to request account support, data correction, or account deletion where applicable.
